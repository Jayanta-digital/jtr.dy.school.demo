History & Overview

A Legacy of Excellence
Since 1948

66no. Radhakuchi LP School is a Government-run Lower Primary School located in Village Radhakuchi, Baihata Chariali Town, Kamrup District, Assam. Established under the Assam Elementary Education Act, the school has been a cornerstone of primary education for over seven decades, serving the children of Radhakuchi and surrounding areas.

The school operates under the supervision of the District Elementary Education Officer (DEEO), Kamrup, and implements the curriculum mandated by SCERT Assam through Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan. Classes are conducted from Class I to Class V in the Assamese medium, with English as a compulsory subject from Class I.

The school actively participates in flagship programs including PM POSHAN (MDM), NIPUN Bharat, Vidyanjali, and implements the National Education Policy 2020 at the primary level.
