About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence
Since 1948

PM Shri 66no. B Radhakuchi J.B. School is a Government-run Lower Primary School located in Radhakuchi Village, Kamrup District. Established under the Assam Elementary Education Act, we have been a cornerstone of primary education in this region for over seven decades.we got selected for the prestigious award of central government the "PM SHRI School" in 2023

The school actively implements government flagship programs including PM POSHAN, NIPUN Bharat, and NEP 2020 guidelines through Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan.
